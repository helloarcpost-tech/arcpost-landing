Yes. Every article is written by Nelson, with AI tools used for research and structure. The final output is reviewed, edited, and human-written. Not a raw AI dump with a light edit.

Every plan includes one free revision per article. Tell us what to change and we rewrite it. No drama, no extra charge.

"Can I choose my own topics?"

Yes. When you brief us, you can specify topics, angles, target keywords, or competitors you want to reference. We work from your direction, not ours.

AI, Tech, and Marketing only. We stay in these three because depth matters more than breadth. If your audience is in one of these spaces, we write for them well.

As shared Google Docs. Formatted, clean, and ready to paste into your CMS or newsletter platform. No attachments, no proprietary formats.
